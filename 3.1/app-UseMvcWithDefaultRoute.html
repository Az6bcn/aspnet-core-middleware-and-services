app.UseMvcWithDefaultRoute


Adds MVC to the IApplicationBuilder request execution pipeline with a default route named 'default' and the following template: '{controller=Home}/{action=Index}/{id?}'. Basic route to your project, which will try to map URL's to methods on one of your controllers
